Every developer once in a while in a career goes this phase of switching technologies for Skill enhancement and ensure longetivity in the domain

Mobile domain is certainly a market to plunder with the recent times. A life of developer is no more restricted to certain technologies or languages since companies are aggressive on making new products and exploring new markets.

There are more ready backend services available in the market:

Amazon Web Services (AWS) - Cloud Computing Services

Microsoft Azure: Cloud Computing Platform & Services

Crisp Takeaways

  • Make yourself strong in Data Structure and Design Patterns
  • Know one Database Management System (Relational / NoSQL)
  • Enhance your iOS development skills
  • Explore on Cloud services offered
  • Learn one scripting language

Never consider language as a barrier, if you are strong in understanding Architecture and Design patterns you could master any language in couple of week.

Since you are in your initial steps of building your career as a developer/architect/entrepreneur , You should start building your expertise across technologies and domain and aim to be a Full Stack Developer
